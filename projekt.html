The FIFA Men's World Ranking is a ranking system for men's national teams in association football, led by Argentina as of April 2023.[1] The teams of the men's member nations of FIFA, football's world governing body, are ranked based on their game results with the most successful teams being ranked highest. The rankings were introduced in December 1992, and eight teams (Argentina, Belgium, Brazil, France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands and Spain) have held the top position, of which Brazil have spent the longest ranked first.

Manchester City

Manchester City Football Club was founded in 1880 under the name St. Mark's West Gorton at the initiative of the members of the church of St. Mark's Church of England , West Gorton, Manchester . Against the background of high unemployment, very large social problems arose, such as alcoholism and violence between certain gangs. Arthur Connell (rector of St. Mark's Church) and his daughter Anne Connel became involved in reducing these problems and therefore created a cricket club in 1875 . In 1880 two churchwardens of St Mark's - William Beastow and Thomas Goodbehere - also started a football team called"St. Mark's West Gorton" . Sometimes this was called West Gorton (St Mark's) .

Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol , commonly known as Real Madrid , or simply Real , is a professional football club based in Madrid , Spain , which plays in Primera División . It is one of the most successful teams in Spanish football and was named by FIFA as "The Best Club of the 20th Century " after winning a record 35 La Liga titles , 19 Spanish Cups, a record 14 UEFA Champions League / European Champions Cup titles and 2 UEFA Cups . Real is a founding member of FIFA and the defunct G-14 group of elite European football clubs..

FC Bayern Munchen

Bayern München Bayern Munich,[4] or simply Bayern, is a German professional sports club based in Munich, Bavaria.Bayern is the most successful club in German football history, having won a record 32 national titles, including 10 consecutively since 2013, and 20 national cups, along with numerous European honours.
